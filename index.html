I am a 3rd year Computer Science Ph.D. student at UC Berkeley. I do research in computational photography and computer graphics with Prof.Ren Ng. I'm interested in image enhancement, editing and synthesis. Before coming to Berkeley, I graduated from Rice University with a B.S. in Electrical Engineering, advised by Prof. Ashok Veeraraghavan.

Research

My research is all about playing around with pixels. I'm interested in photo enhancement, editing and synthesis. I want to design algorithms and software to make high-quality photography more accessible to people.
During the past few years, I'm fortunate to have collaborated with these inspiring people from the industrial research labs: Joon-Young Lee (Adobe), Kalyan Sunkavalli (Adobe), Zhaowen Wang (Adobe), Kevin Matzen (Facebook), Qifeng Chen (Intel).
